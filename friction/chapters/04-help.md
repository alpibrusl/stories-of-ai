# Help

> **Outline stage — beat sheet, not finished prose.**

The irreversible cost (issue #13). Something goes genuinely wrong *because* Nora
insisted on the unmanaged way — a real, lasting consequence (candidate: an injury
on the long way, a lost chance Tomás never knew he had, a fracture with Miguel). The
system was right, and gentle, and would have prevented it. For once in the
anthology, refusing help is what causes the harm — and it indicts Nora, not the
machine. No villain; a real wound. Keep it human-scale and specific, never
melodramatic.
