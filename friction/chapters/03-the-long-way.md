# The Long Way

> **Outline stage — beat sheet, not finished prose.**

Nora deliberately gives Tomás one hard, unoptimized experience — a real journey, a
real failure, a thing built that breaks. It is awkward; he half-engages. One moment
of genuine, unmanaged connection between them. And the first clear sign that he
experiences the not-knowing as a curiosity his mother enjoys, not a need he shares.
