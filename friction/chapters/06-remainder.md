# Remainder

> **Outline stage — beat sheet, not finished prose.**

Coda, year ~28. Nora is older; she has kept one domain unmanaged — the hard thing
she still does herself. Adult Tomás calls: he has been asked to join *a committee*,
something about what the optimized world might have lost, and he wants to ask her —
what was it like, before? She finds she cannot explain it, the way Gérard cannot
explain his methodology, the way the documentation does not cover it.

End on the live, unanswerable question between mother and grown son — and a small,
hard-won thing that holds. No quiet resignation. **No server hum.** This is the
threshold of the man EIGHT MINUTES will meet, fifteen years on.
