# Opt Out

> **Outline stage — beat sheet, not finished prose.**

Nora declines — tries to keep one domain of Tomás's life unmanaged. She discovers
that declining is allowed, frictionless, and hollow: the world simply routes around
her *no*. Tomás becomes the only child doing things the hard way, and it isolates
him without ennobling him. (Deliberate echo of AGREEABLE's "you can decline, of
course.") End on the first cost: his isolation, and her doubt.
