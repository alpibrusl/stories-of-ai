# Before

> **Outline stage — beat sheet, not finished prose.** See `../outline.md` and the
> chapter-1 beat in `../bible.yaml`.

Lisbon, year ~15. Establish Nora through one specific memory of friction she once
lived through and was changed by — a hard thing, an uncertain outcome, the
particular quality of attention that only comes when it might go wrong. Establish
Tomás, 9, who has never known anything like it, and the easy fluency with which he
moves through a world that has always worked.

The inciting offer: the system proposes, politely, to optimize Tomás's development
— pre-answer his questions, smooth his frustrations, route him around failure.
Miguel sees no reason to decline. Nora's unease has no argument behind it yet, only
a body that knows something. End on the quiet, first divergence between her and
Miguel.
