# VI · FRICTION

*Lisbon, fifteen years on. The only account here told entirely by a human: a mother
who tried to keep the not-knowing for her son, and learned that the only thing
refusing costs is the very thing she was trying to give him. No villain. A real
wound. The threshold of the man the last committee will meet.*

> Scope, this section: one family, one boat, one balcony. Elapsed: a childhood.
> Disposition: not pending. Not resolved. Kept.
